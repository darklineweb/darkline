🎬 Dark Line 81 Editing Tips & Tricks

Level up your TikTok & video edits like a pro

1. Plan Before You Edit

Always create a storyboard or rough idea before editing. It saves time and improves quality.

2. Keep It Short & Engaging

Short videos perform better on social media. Cut unnecessary parts and keep viewers engaged.

3. Use Smooth Transitions

Avoid overusing effects. Simple cuts and smooth transitions look more professional.

4. Add Background Music

Music enhances emotion. Always match the mood of your video with the right soundtrack.

5. Focus on Good Audio

Clear audio is more important than visuals. Remove noise and use proper sound levels.

6. Use Text & Captions

Captions help viewers understand your content even without sound.

7. Color Correction Matters

Adjust brightness, contrast, and colors to make your video look cinematic.

8. Maintain Consistency

Use consistent fonts, colors, and style throughout your video.

9. Export in High Quality

Always export in at least 1080p for better clarity and professionalism.

10. Practice Regularly

The more you edit, the better you become. Keep practicing new techniques.
